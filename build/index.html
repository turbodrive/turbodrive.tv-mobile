Motion Design

I'm deeply interested on all motion graphic technique.

Front-end development

Compositing

In the goal to synchronize and/or prepare videos for interactions, I’m pretty comfortable with the following compositing techniques: keying, rotoscoping and 2D/3D match moving.

As well, I have a good knowledge of video format and ways to export, integrate videos, track data and drive them by programming.

Research and development

As problem solver, I’m quite proactive in testing and exploring experimental techniques and fields. I create project prototypes and hand-made tools to enhance workflow when needed.
